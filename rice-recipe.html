Rice

Rice is the ultimate student staple, but who has time to wait for a pot to boil? With this microwave hack, you'll have perfectly fluffy rice ready to fuel your study sessions in minutes‚Äîno stove required üçö.

Total time

12 min.

serving

1 serving

Instructions

step 1 (optional)

Measure 1/2 cup of rice into a microwave-safe bowl. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.

step 2

Pour 1 cup of water into the bowl with the rinsed rice. The ratio of water to rice is crucial for perfect results, so don't skip this step!

step 3

Cover the bowl with a microwave-safe lid or plate or a paper towel. This helps trap steam and ensures even cooking.

step 4

Cook the rice on high for 5 minutes. Then, carefully remove the bowl from the microwave and give the rice a quick stir.

step 5

Return the bowl to the microwave and cook for an additional 5 minutes. If you're making a larger batch, adjust the cooking time accordingly.

ingredients

1/2 cup (120ml) of rice

1 cup (240ml) of water

tools

Microwave-saved bowl

Microwave-saved lid/plate/paper towel

A cup to measure rice
