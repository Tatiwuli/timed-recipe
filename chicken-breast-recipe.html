Chicken Breast

No need to use the stove neither oil to enjoy a juicy chicken breast. With a microwave and a few minutes, you can whip up a satisfying protein fix that's perfect for any busy uni schedule!

Total time

12 min.

serving

1 serving

Instructions

step 1

Place a boneless, skinless chicken breast on a microwave-safe plate and pat it dry with a paper towel. This helps the seasoning stick better and ensures even cooking.

step 2

Sprinkle both sides of the chicken breast with your favorite seasoning blend. Whether it's simple salt and pepper or a dash of your secret spice mix, make it your own!

step 3

Cook the chicken on high for 3-4 minutes per side. After cooking both sides, check if it's ready by cutting into the thickest part.

step 4

Let the chicken rest for a minute after cooking. This allows the juices to redistribute, keeping the meat tender and juicy.

ingredients

1 boneless, skinless chicken breast (the quantity is based on preference)

Any seasoning: salt, black pepper, paprika, basil, shoyu

tools

Microwave-safe bowl/plate

Kitchen towel(optional)

Enjoy!
