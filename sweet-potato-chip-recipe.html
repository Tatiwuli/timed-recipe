Sweet Potato Chips

With just a potato and a microwave, you can whip up crispy, golden chips that'll satisfy your snack cravings in minutes.

Total time

12 min.

serving

1 serving

Instructions

step 1

Wash and dry a medium-sized potato. Using a sharp knife or a mandoline slicer, slice the potato into thin, uniform slices. The thinner, the crispier!

step 2

Lay the seasoned potato slices in a single layer on a microwave-safe plate. Make sure they're not overlapping to ensure even cooking.

step 3

Cook the potato slices in the microwave on high for 3-4 minutes. Keep an eye on themâ€”they can go from golden to burnt quickly!

step 4

Carefully flip the potato slices over and return them to the microwave. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until they're golden brown and crispy.

step 5

Let the potato chips cool on the plate for a minute or two. They'll continue to crisp up as they cool down.

ingredients

1 medium sweet potato/potato

Any seasonings: Salt, pepper, basil

Any oil (ideally olive)

tools

Microwave-saved plate

Paper Towel to dry the potato

Enjoy the snack or add an extra crunch to your salad!
