Scrambled Eggs

A simple and nutritious meal that can fuel your entire morning and goes perfectly with toast, burrito wrap, or even solo! Friednly for those who need extra protein too üòÅ.

Total time

4 min

serving

1 serving

Instructions

step 1

Crack 2 eggs into a microwave-safe bowl.

step 2

Add a splash of milk (about 1 tablespoon) and whisk until well combined.

step 3

Season with salt and pepper to taste.

step 4

Microwave on high for 30 seconds, then stir.

step 5

Continue to microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring between each interval, until eggs reach to your desired consistency..

Optional step - adding cheese

Sprinkle cheese on top and microwave for another 15 seconds, until cheese is melted.

ingredients

1-2 eggs

Milk (for more fluffy texture)

Salt, pepper to taste

tools

Microwave-saved bowl

Anything to stir the eggs

Enjoy and have a wonderful day!
